# SEO and GEO Plan

## Objectives
- Rank for high-intent service queries related to AI workflow stabilization and internal tools.
- Attract qualified small business leads, with emphasis on e-commerce/Amazon operations.
- Keep service information easy to parse for search engines and LLMs.

## Audience and Intent
- Business owners and operators with fragile AI and automation workflows
- Teams considering whether to keep patching prompts/automations or build software
- Buyers looking for clear pricing ranges and practical implementation

## Suggested Page Targeting
- Hero + intro: broad positioning keyword cluster
- Offer section: audit, stabilization sprint, internal tool build clusters
- Proof section: credibility and use-case matching queries
- FAQ section: long-tail questions and objections

## On-Page SEO Checklist
- One clear H1 with primary service intent
- Semantic heading hierarchy (`h2`/`h3`) by service topic
- Descriptive title and meta description (EN and DE variants)
- Internal section anchors for scannability
- High-quality alt text for proof screenshots
- FAQ phrased as direct question/answer pairs

## GEO / LLM Readability Checklist
- Use short factual paragraphs
- Add bullet summaries in each offer block
- State explicit constraints (scope, outcomes, pricing ranges)
- Keep terminology consistent (audit, stabilization sprint, internal tool)
- Add JSON-LD where relevant (Person, Service, FAQPage)

## Measurement and Validation
- Track key conversion: click on `Book a call`
- Track secondary conversions: contact clicks and proof item interactions
- Validate metadata and structured data after deployment
- Run Lighthouse and manual extraction checks (prompt tests)
